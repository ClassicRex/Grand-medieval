# Classic_Rex's Grand Medieval


## 📜 The Story

The age of peace has ended. From frozen peaks to burning deserts, the Great Drakes have returned. Armed with nothing but a rusted blade, you must venture into a world where the sky is ruled by fire. Will you be a dragon's lunch, or forge their scales into your own legend?

## ⚔️ Core Gameplay

The Spartan Arsenal: Master 30+ new weapon types like Halberds and Warhammers with unique reach and speed.

The Age of Dragons: Hunt Fire, Ice, and Lightning dragons in massive caverns with Ice and Fire.

Souls-like Combat: Use Epic Fight for a dedicated combat mode featuring dodges, rolls, and heavy attacks.

Total Immersion GUI: Every menu is transformed into a medieval aesthetic of parchment, wood, and iron.

Infinite Discovery: Explore 100+ mods featuring sprawling dungeons and castles from When Dungeons Arise.

## ✨ Visuals & Tech

Living World: Over 80+ new animals and monsters via Alex's Mobs and Born in Chaos.

Cinematic Atmosphere: Bliss Shaders provide dynamic fog and realistic metallic reflections on your armor.

 Performance: Optimized with Embeddium for smooth FPS at 6GB–8GB RAM.
